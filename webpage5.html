How you can do Multiple Cropping?
There are different forms and types of multiple cropping.
Intercropping: Intercropping means interspersion of different crops on the same place of land, such as a home garden, and alternate rows usually designed to minimize competition but maximize the potential for both crops to make use of the available nutrients.
Rotations: the growing of two or more crops in sequence on the same piece of land.
Agroforestry: Annual herbaceous crops are grown interspersed with perennial trees or shrubs.
Green Manuring: the growing of legumes and other plants to fix nitrogen and then incorporate the nutrients into the soil.
